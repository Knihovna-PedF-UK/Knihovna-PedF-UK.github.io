Study Room

The Study Room is located on the ground floor to the left of porter’s room. It provides books and journals, there are also computers with also Internet connection.

The computers may be used for browsing the Internet and the digital resources available for the library. It is possible to use the computers for writing seminar papers and other school works. You can also connect to the Internet using your own device via the WiFi network. Printer, scanner, a copy machine or ring-binding are available for use.

Additional provided services include borrowing USB charger, USB cable, flash drive or e-book reader in the study room.
