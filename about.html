About Library

Address: Magdalény Rettigové 4, 116 39 Prague 1

There are two branches of our Library in this building – the reference desk (loans) and the study room (printing, web browsing, in-house loans).

Location:

Reference desk: ground floor to the left of the porter’s room

Study room: ground floor to the left of the porter’s room

Our library is barrier-free. There is a deck system to help disabled users to get into the building. We also have a barrier-free toilette in front of the library.
