News

05.01.2026 – Library Renovation: Temporary Closure and Service Changes

During the renovation, we are expanding the space available for study and meetings. Consultations are provided in office R136 on Mondays and Wednesdays from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., or by email at knihovna@pedf.cuni.cz. The study room at Celetná 13 remains open under normal conditions.

During the closure:

  • the lending desk and study room at M. Rettigové 4 are closed,
  • loans due during this period are automatically extended until 4 May 2026,
  • books can be returned at any time via the book drop at the porter’s desk,
  • borrowing books (including via reservation boxes) is not possible.

For up-to-date information, please follow the faculty website and social media. Thank you for your understanding, and we look forward to welcoming you to our new spaces after the renovation.

16.09.2025 – We will reopen the library at M. Rettigová 4 on 29 September. The reading room at Celetná 13 is open daily.

The loan period for all books has been extended until 15 October 2025. The reading room at Celetná 13 is open Monday to Thursday 9:00–16:00 and Friday 9:00–13:00. We look forward to seeing you!

09.06.2025 – New media database NewtonOne

The Anopress media database has now been replaced by the more modern NewtonOne platform.

The entire user interface has been redesigned – it’s more user-friendly and offers new features, such as grouping news about the same event and tracking article edits, including headline changes.

Try NewtonOne at home, on the go, or in our library – we’ll be happy to help!
